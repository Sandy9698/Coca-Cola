Limca® Book of Records

India is a powerhouse of talent, vigour, and ingenuity in every possible arena. Reflecting India's multifaceted progression towards an ever-evolving future, the LimcaBook of Records has been a veritable chronicle of the country's achievements since its inception in 1987.

Continuing its theme of 'India at her Best’, the 2024 edition exhibits records from various fields etched in the history of time. From the record-breaking show at the Asian Games 2022 to the launch of Aditya L-1, India’s first space-based solar observatory to the newly designed Sansad Bhavan, the 33rd Limca Book of Records celebrates stories of perseverance, patience, and performances.

Owned by Coca‑Cola India Private Limited, which manufactures the Indian beverage Limca, the LBR is a time-tested legacy that recognises the indomitable spirit of humanity, encourages the pursuit of dreams, and harnesses limitless potential.

With a wide range of old and new records, reader-friendly infographics, at-a-glance tables and charts, and carefully curated reference information, this edition stands true to its own legacy as India's first-ever record book.
